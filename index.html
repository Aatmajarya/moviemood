What is MovieMood?

MovieMood is an AI-powered movie discovery tool that removes the "what should I watch?" stress forever. Instead of endlessly scrolling through streaming platforms, you simply tell MovieMood how you feel, set a few filters, and instantly get 5 handpicked movie suggestions perfectly tailored to your mood, language, and vibe.

💡

Why I Built This

We've all been there — 30 minutes spent scrolling Netflix looking for something to watch, only to give up and rewatch something old. The problem isn't the lack of movies, it's the lack of the right movie for the right moment. MovieMood was built to solve exactly that. By combining your emotional state with smart filters, it cuts through the noise and delivers movies that actually match how you feel right now.

How MovieMood Works 🎯

Find Your Right Movie

5 simple steps to your perfect movie night

1

Pick Your Mood 🎭

Start by selecting how you feel right now — Happy, Sad, Romantic, or Nostalgic. Your mood is the heart of every recommendation MovieMood makes.

😄
2

Set Your Filters 🎛️

Choose your Language, Industry (Bollywood, Hollywood, Tollywood), Genre, how much time you have, and who you're watching with. All filters are optional.

⚙️
3

Hit "Find My Movie" 🚀

Click the button and our AI instantly processes your mood and filters to craft a personalised recommendation just for you. No two searches are ever the same.

🎬
4

Get 5 Personalised Picks 🍿

Scroll through 5 handpicked movie suggestions — each with title, year, rating, synopsis, and a reason why it matches your exact mood and filters.

✨
5

Find Where to Watch 📺

Click "Where to Watch" on any card and we'll take you straight to JustWatch — which shows every streaming platform the movie is available on right now.

📺

Ready to find your perfect movie? 🎬
